# IMRaD Outline

## Title

- Specific object of study
- Method or result signal
- No inflated claim

## Abstract

1. Problem
2. Gap
3. Method
4. Main result with quantitative detail
5. Interpretation and limitation

## Introduction

- Field context
- Specific gap
- Why the gap matters
- Contributions

## Methods

- Design
- Data/sample/materials
- Procedure
- Outcomes/metrics
- Analysis plan
- Reproducibility details

## Results

- Primary result first
- Secondary results
- Robustness or sensitivity analyses
- Negative or null results

## Discussion

- What the results mean
- How they compare with prior work
- Limitations
- Implications
- Future work

## Conclusion

- One calibrated paragraph.
- No new evidence.
