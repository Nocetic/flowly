# Recommendation Scale

Use only when a recommendation is requested.

## Accept

The claims are important, evidence is strong, methods are sound, and remaining issues are editorial or minor.

## Minor Revision

The main claims are supported, but the artifact needs clarification, additional reporting, small analyses, or better positioning.

## Major Revision

The work may be valuable, but important claims require additional evidence, stronger baselines, clearer methods, corrected statistics, or reproducibility fixes.

## Reject

The central claim is unsupported, methods cannot answer the question, novelty is insufficient, conclusions are misleading, or reproducibility gaps are too large to evaluate.

## Insufficient Information

Use when the artifact is incomplete or key materials are missing. State exactly what is needed to review it.
